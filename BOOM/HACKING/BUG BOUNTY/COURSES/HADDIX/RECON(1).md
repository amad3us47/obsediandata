
Recon is not a linear process. Good recon has a lot of "jumping" around.
Find the "apex domains" again and again and subdomains .
find the subdomains of apex domains
find lots of attack surface.
do lot of recon.
do regular recon on a target.

# TIPS
1. Find the responsible disclosure in their site(main site) or in the scope on the hacking platform.
2. HTTP(without https) domains also good
3. Get the IP ranges of all surface
4. Get the [[Acquisition]]
5. Using founder email address also good to find the apex domains
6. Analytics Relationships https://builtwith.com/ (Good for finding phishing domains)
7. Get all the root and live domains from all the tools and then feed it to (puredns, altdns)
8. Tech profiling (wappalyzer, whatruns, builtwith)
9. Play with the app just start using it
10. Content discovery using recursive option
