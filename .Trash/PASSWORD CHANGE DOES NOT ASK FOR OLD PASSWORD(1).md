So,in this particular case,if someone forgets to logout from wordpress after using on shared computer, or think that while he was using wordpress on some others computer at that time something happens with his connection.That time he will be unable to logout from wordpress .And his account will be in deep danger. Cause anyone who have access to the account can change the account email address/password and takeover the account. As the process does not require to enter his old password for verification.
